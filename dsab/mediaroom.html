We work closely with the media to provide informative materials regarding the prevention of Shaken Baby Syndrome.

Here you will find media resources on our campaign which kicked off Monday April 10, 2006.

We believe that in order to dramatically impact this issue and reduce the problem, education and awareness is key. Child abuse can be prevented and this program is an important step in taking action.

We would be happy to respond to inquiries, provide you with additional information and quality graphics.

Please contact:

The Children's Hospital: Natalie Goldstein, Media Relations: 303-861-3970 Goldstein.Natalie@tchden.org

Kempe: Lindsey Zimmerman, Communications: 303-864-5306 Zimmerman.Lindsey@Kempe.org

Rocky Mountain PBS
as a statewide public service to the community, Rocky Mountain PBS (Ch.6) aired the 30-minute award-winning documentary: "Shaken Baby: Elijah's Story" - that follows the life of a 16-month-old who was shaken to death by his biological father when he became frustrated with Elijah's crying.

To order your copy visit: The Shaken Baby Center
